# Privacy Policy for Mind Influence Lab

**Last Updated:** February 2026

## 1. Introduction

Mind Influence Lab ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Sensor Data (Local Only)
Our app accesses the following device sensors **solely for real-time experiment processing and entropy generation**:
- **Magnetometer** - Measures magnetic field strength
- **Accelerometer** - Detects device movement and stillness

**Important:** All sensor data is processed **locally on your device** and is **NOT transmitted to any external server** unless you explicitly choose to share it.

### 2.2 System Entropy Data (For RNG)
The app uses Android's cryptographically secure random number generator, which collects entropy from:
- Sensor noise readings
- System timing variations
- User interaction timing

**This entropy data is NOT stored** — it is used only for random number generation and immediately discarded.

### 2.3 Session Data (Stored Locally)
The app stores the following data **only on your device**:
- Random Number Generator (RNG) results
- Magnetic field readings
- Acceleration/movement data
- Session context (energy level, stress level, intention, mood tags)
- User-added session notes

### 2.4 What We DO NOT Collect
- ❌ No personal identification information
- ❌ No location data
- ❌ No contact information
- ❌ No browsing history
- ❌ No data sold to third parties
- ❌ No analytics or tracking services

## 3. How We Use Your Data

All collected data is used **exclusively** for:
- Displaying real-time experiment feedback
- Generating session reports and insights
- Tracking your personal research progress
- Identifying patterns in your experimental sessions
- System entropy generation for RNG (not stored)

## 4. Data Storage

- All data is stored **locally on your device**
- Data is stored in the app's private storage directory
- You can delete any session data at any time from the "Research Journey" screen
- Uninstalling the app will remove all stored data

## 5. Data Sharing

### 5.1 Voluntary Sharing
You may **optionally** share session data via:
- Share button in session results (exports CSV file)
- You control what, when, and with whom you share

### 5.2 No Automatic Sharing
- We do NOT automatically upload your data
- We do NOT share data with advertisers
- We do NOT share data with analytics services
- We do NOT share data with third parties
